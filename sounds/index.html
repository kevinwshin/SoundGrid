Hey, this plays music (hopefully), turn your sound on.

Click a cell to toggle it on and off. Each column represents a chromatic scale starting with C at the bottom and increasing in pitch to C at the top. Additionally, each column also represents a beat in a 12 beat measure.

The state of the grid is stored on the server, so you should gather your friends and compose collaboratively.

The up/down arrow keys control the volume. Click anywhere to dismiss this popup.
